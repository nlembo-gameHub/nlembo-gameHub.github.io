About Me

My name is Nathan Lembo, a Gameplay Programmer and someone with a passion for Game and Level Design. I have a love for designing and creating player interactions with the environment and game world. As a Gameplay Programmer, developing events and locations filled with interactivity and life is something that I strive for in my work and consistently aim to provide so that players can feel and experience the environment and its details. Ever since I was young I've been captivated by video games with the worlds and experiences it created and this pushed me to take my own path towards Game Development. I'm a graduate student at WakeTechnical Community College and I am currently a student at William Peace University for a Bachelor's in Game Design. Through my higher education I've gained experience in working with multiple game engines such as Unreal 4 and Unity as well as working with teams to create games and projects. With these skills and experiences I strive to create and work with others to create fun environments and provide players with opportunities to explore and interact with the worlds created for them.
